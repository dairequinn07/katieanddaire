Wedding location & travel tips

What airport to fly into?

Two main airports you can travel into are Milan Bergamo or Verona. Both are very similar when it comes to travelling to Gardone. You can fly from Belfast or Dublin to Bergamo and Verona is only from Dublin. Some flights are available from Dublin and Belfast. £128 return from Dublin to Bergamo not including luggage. To travel from Verona or Milan Bergamo to Gardone Riviera on Lake Garda, you have several transportation options.

By car

Car Rental: At the Milan Bergamo Airport, you can rent a car from one of the many car rental agencies. We recently rented a Skoda jeep from Rental Plus. Cost us £150 for a week rental and we had no issues. Journey is < 1 hour from Bergamo to Gardone and it's basically a straight road!!

By taxi (very few so book in advance)

  • Alla: She has a few taxi drivers under her, so very helpful. +39 327 449 0662
  • Roberto: +39 338 365 9218
  • Radio Taxi: +39 0365 43516
  • Lake Garda Transfers: Mr Giuseppe Cipani: +39 329 7108261 lakegardatransfers.co.uk

Tips:

  • Timetables: Check the schedules for trains and buses in advance, especially if traveling during weekends or holidays.
  • Luggage: If you have a lot of luggage, the car rental option might be more convenient.
  • Exploring the lake: If you plan to explore the lake such as visiting other towns like Limone and Sirmione which we recommend, then car is best option. On our last vist to Lake Garda we drove all of the West side of the Lake and we LOVED it. This is why I recommend a car so you can explore the lake at your own leisure however Ferry's also all go around the lake.
  • Lake Garda Transfers: Mr Giuseppe Cipani: +39 329 7108261 lakegardatransfers.co.uk

With these options, you can choose the most suitable and comfortable way to travel from Milan Bergamo to Gardone Riviera on Lake Garda. Enjoy your journey and the stunning views along the way!

Other parts of the lake to visit

If you have time, we really strongly advise to visit other parts of the Lake in your free time! Below are our favourite towns that are a MUST visit!!

  • Sirmione
  • The most popular tourist destination on the south shore, known for its castle and thermal baths.
  • Malcesine
  • The pearl of Lake Garda, located in a picturesque setting with a cable car to Monte Baldo.
  • Limone Sul Garda
  • A charming town with lemon groves and a scenic promenade.
  • Riva Del Garda
  • A paradise for adventure lovers, with windsurfing, sailing, hiking, and biking opportunities.
  • Desenzano
  • A lively town with a historic center, a harbor, and a nightlife.
  • Gardaland
  • Theme park for the kids and the adults :)
